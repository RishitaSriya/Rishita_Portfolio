THUMBNAILS FOLDER
=================
Put your .jpg or .png images here.

For video card posters (shown before video plays):
  Use the same name as your video for easy tracking.
  Example: reel-001.jpg for reel-001.mp4

For thumbnail design cards (the work you show off):
  thumb-design-1.jpg
  thumb-design-2.jpg
  etc.

In index.html, find <img> tags and update:
  src="thumbnails/YOUR-IMAGE.jpg"
